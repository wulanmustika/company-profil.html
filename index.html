About Us

RedDoorz is a leading hotel chain in Southeast Asia that provides quality accommodation at affordable prices. With more than thousands of properties in different cities, we are here to ensure the comfort and satisfaction of your stay, whether for business or leisure travel.

Our mission is to provide a comfortable, clean and affordable stay for everyone. We are committed to making your journey easier by providing high-quality services that can be accessed wherever you are.

Our vision is to be the first choice for reliable lodging in Southeast Asia, by presenting a hotel network that is easily accessible, comfortable, and consistent in providing the best service.

Our Values

We uphold the values of trust, comfort, and quality. Every guest is our top priority, and we always strive to create a friendly and safe environment, with sincere and professional service.
